6. Notification Flows
NOTIFICATION TRIGGERS & DESTINATIONS:
Event: Student marks lesson complete
  → Activity log updated (silent, no notification)
Event: Quiz deadline approaches (24 hours before)
  → Push notification to student
  → Email to student
Event: Trainer submits attendance
  → Push to all students in batch ("Attendance for [date] marked")
  → If student attendance < 75%: additional warning push
Event: Assignment graded
  → Push + email to student ("Your [Assignment Name] has been graded: X/Y marks")
Event: Certificate generated
  → Push + email to student ("Your [Course] certificate is ready!")
Event: New job posted (matching skills)
  → Push to matching students
Event: Weekly report (Sunday 8 PM auto)
  → Email to all college admins (their college stats)
  → Email to super admin (platform-wide stats)
Event: Student at risk (weekly check)
  → Email to super admin + college admin listing at-risk students
